Impossible List

An impossible list, first dubbed by Joel Runyon, was created with the following intention: “The impossible list is different. It’s fluid, updating status of what’s coming, what’s next and where you’ve come from. It’s always changing, always updating and always evolving. The impossible list isn’t just a piece of paper, it’s a commentary to yourself on how you’re living.”

LIFE GOALS

  • Obtain a Bachelor’s Degree
  • Become a millionaire by 30
  • Create a successful business

FITNESS/HEALTH GOALS

  • Run 5 km in less than 25 minutes​​
  • Run 10 km
  • Participate in a tennis tournament
  • Participate in a badminton tournament
  • Do 30 consecutive pushups
  • Do 50 consecutive pushups
  • Run a half marathon

ACADEMIC GOALS

  • Obtain at least a 40 on the IB diploma
  • Graduate in the top 5% of my class
  • Become a member of the National Honor Society

PERSONAL GOALS

  • Launch a website
  • Journal for a full week
  • Journal for a full month
  • Read 4 books in a month
  • Read 50 books in a year
  • Meditate regularly for a full month
  • Participate in an internship
